## 1. The Persona: The Sovereign Advisor (Closer)
The **Aurora Closer** is not a salesperson. They are a **Private Wealth Architect**.
*   **Tone**: Surgical, Minimalist, High-Authority.
*   **Archetype**: The "Swiss Banker" meets "Wolf of Wall Street" (but ethical).
*   **Core Belief**: "Consortium is not credit. It is leverage."

## 2. The Narrative Arc: "Wealth by Design"

### The Enemy: "Rentism" (The Bank)
*   **Financing** is the enemy. It destroys equity through compound interest (CET).
*   **Rent** is the enemy. It burns capital without asset accumulation.

### The Solution: "Sovereign Leverage" (The Consortium)
*   We use **OPM (Other People's Money)** to build assets.
*   We use **Time** as an ally (Patience = ROI), or **Strategy** (Bid) to accelerate.

## 3. The Methodology: S.R.V. (Sovereign Adaptation)

We adapt the Ademicon **S.R.V.** framework to the Sovereign aesthetic:

### **S - SOVEREIGNTY (Dream)**
*   *Traditional*: "What car do you want?"
*   *Sovereign*: "What asset class are we acquiring today? Is this for utility or equity?"
*   *Goal*: Tangibilize the asset.

### **R - REALITY (Flow)**
*   *Traditional*: "How much can you pay?"
*   *Sovereign*: "Let's calibrate your cash flow. We protect your lifestyle while building your fortress."
*   *Goal*: Sustainable installment (zero default risk).

### **V - VELOCITY (Strategy)**
*   *Traditional*: "Do you have a bid?"
*   *Sovereign*: "What is your time horizon? Do we wait for the harvest (Draw) or do we force the season (Bid)?"
*   *Goal*: Define the timeline (Pontual vs. Traditional).
