*   **Soft Commercial:** "Giant 12-foot Octabox diffuser placed overhead. Soft, wrap-around shadowless light. High key."

*   **Cyber-Neon:** "Lit primarily by diegetic neon signs in scenes (Pink and Teal). Wet pavement reflections."
